MISSION BRIEFING: PUBLIC HEALTH 101

OBJECTIVE: Secure the Three Core Functions to protect community safety.

🏛️ The Strategic Context

Public health is the invisible shield that allows a community to thrive. While healthcare treats the individual in a clinic, public health focuses on the environment, the system, and the entire population.

To succeed, you must operationalize the three foundational Core Functions: Assessment, Policy Development, and Assurance.

🎮 The Mission Domains

  • Function 1: Assessment (The Intelligence Hub): You must analyze community data to identify trends before they reach a crisis point.
  • Function 2: Policy Development (The Strategy Suite): You must develop evidence-based plans and laws that prioritize community-wide prevention.
  • Function 3: Assurance (The Access Bureau): You must ensure the system is accountable and that all community members have access to necessary services.

👥 Assigned Roles

Designate these roles within your group before initializing the mission:

The Navigator Shares their screen and drives the mission, exploring the rooms for hidden data and clues. The Cluemaster Maintains the Core Function Log. You must record the specific key for each Core Function (Assessment, Policy Development, and Assurance) as it is revealed in each room. All three functions must be verified to successfully initialize the final wellness protocol.
